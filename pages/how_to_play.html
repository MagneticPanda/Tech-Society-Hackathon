How to Play

Snake is a game where you control a snake and try to eat as many apples as possible. The snake will grow longer as you eat more apples. The game ends when the snake hits the wall or itself. The goal is to get the highest score possible.

There are two modes of play: Classic and Time Attack. In Classic mode, you have unlimited time to eat as many apples as possible. In Time Attack mode, you have 60 seconds to eat as many apples as possible. The snake will move faster in Time Attack mode.

There are also two difficulty levels: Easy and Hard. In Easy mode, the snake will move slower. In Hard mode, the snake will move faster.

There are also two game modes: Single Player and Multiplayer. In Single Player mode, you play against the computer. In Multiplayer mode, you play against another player.

Scoring

Each apple eaten will give you 10 points. The score will be displayed at the top of the screen.

Type of Items

Click the Reload Game button to reload the game. This will reset the game and start a new game.

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move the snake. Use the space bar to pause the game.

Leaderboard

The leaderboard will display the top 10 scores for each game mode and difficulty level. The leaderboard will be updated after each game.

Game Over

The game will end when the snake hits the wall or itself. The game will display your score and ask you to enter your name. Your score will be added to the leaderboard.
